Best Pet DNA Tests: Compare the Top Dog DNA Kits

Find out which DNA test is right for your dog! We’ve reviewed and ranked the top five dog DNA test kits to help you make an informed decision.

Top Dog DNA Test Kits

1. ProsperK9 ★★★★★

Our Top Pick: ProsperK9 offers advanced breed identification, health screenings, and behavior insights. Ideal for professionals and pet owners alike.

2. Embark ★★★★☆

Embark provides highly accurate breed identification and comprehensive health screenings. One of the largest canine genetic databases makes this kit stand out.

3. Basepaws ★★★★☆

Basepaws offers reliable breed identification and genetic health markers. Great for pet owners who want to understand both breed and health risks.

4. Wisdom Panel ★★★★☆

Wisdom Panel provides extensive breed identification and health reports. A trusted brand but with less detailed health insights compared to competitors.

5. EpiPaws ★★★☆☆

EpiPaws is focused on genetic health markers and is suitable for pet owners primarily interested in health but lacks detailed breed insights.
